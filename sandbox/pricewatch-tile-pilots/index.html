PriceWatch / Sector Identity System

Artifact-led front faces for non-Energy, non-Metals tiles

The illustration layer is removed. These pilots treat each front face as a trade artifact: certificate stock, sack print, grading label, cold-chain carton, or mill stamp. The system uses typography, pattern, border logic, substrate treatment, and print detail rather than bespoke drawings.

Agriculture

Warm certificate stock, export-label framing, seed-row logic, and mercantile descriptors. Grains lean archival and institutional, oilseeds lean processed and press-coded, softs lean crate-marked and port-facing.

Fertilizers

Assay-sheet and sack-print language: ledger grids, notation bands, stencil weight, and industrial shorthand. The personality is technical and traded, not academic chemistry.

Livestock, Dairy & Seafood

Auction tags, grading cards, inspection blocks, and cold-chain carton marks. Meats feel lot-graded and boxed; seafood feels netted, shipped, and refrigerated rather than retail.

Forest & Wood Products

Mill-stamp simplicity: board-grade structure, saw-line rhythm, growth-ring emblem, and durable certification hierarchy. It should read as the most materially direct sector.
